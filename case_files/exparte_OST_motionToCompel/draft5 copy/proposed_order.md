### [PROPOSED] ORDER GRANTING PLAINTIFF’S EX PARTE APPLICATION FOR ORDER SHORTENING TIME ON PLAINTIFF’S MOTION TO COMPEL DEFENDANT’S DEPOSITION AND FOR CONDENSED BRIEFING SCHEDULE

The Ex Parte Application of Plaintiff Michael James Romero for an Order Shortening Time on Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Defendant’s Deposition and for a condensed briefing schedule came on for hearing on November 13, 2025 at 9:00 a.m. in Dept. R12 of the above‑entitled Court.

Having considered the Application, supporting declarations and exhibits, and the arguments presented, and good cause appearing, the Court orders as follows:

1.  **Ex parte application granted (scheduling relief only).**  
    This Order grants scheduling relief only. The Court does not decide the merits of the Motion, rule on any request for sanctions, or grant/deny any discovery or evidentiary relief at the ex parte hearing.

2.  **Hearing on underlying Motion advanced.**  
    The hearing on Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Defendant’s Deposition, currently set for February 11, 2026 at 9:00 a.m. in Dept. R12, is advanced and specially set for:
    - **Date:** __________________________
    - **Time:** _________ a.m./p.m.
    - **Dept.:** R12
    - **Courthouse:** 8303 Haven Avenue, Rancho Cucamonga, California 91730

    The advanced hearing will proceed as a regular noticed motion hearing on the Motion.

3.  **Opposition deadline.**  
    Any Opposition to the Motion shall be filed and served no later than five (5) court days before the advanced hearing date.

4.  **Reply deadline.**  
    Any Reply shall be filed and served no later than two (2) court days before the advanced hearing date.

5.  **Service of this Order and the Motion.**  
    Plaintiff shall promptly serve this Order, together with the Motion and supporting papers (to the extent not previously served), on Defendant’s counsel in a manner reasonably calculated to provide actual notice before the advanced hearing, and in accordance with the Code of Civil Procedure, the California Rules of Court, and any applicable local rules.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
