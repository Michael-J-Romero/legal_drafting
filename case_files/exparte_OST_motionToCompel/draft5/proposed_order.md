[PROPOSED] ORDER GRANTING PLAINTIFF'S EX PARTE APPLICATION
---------------------------------------------------------

FOR ORDER SHORTENING TIME ON PLAINTIFF'S MOTION TO COMPEL DEFENDANT'S DEPOSITION; REQUEST FOR MONETARY SANCTIONS; AND REQUEST FOR LIMITED LEAVE AFTER DISCOVERY CUTOFF; AND FOR CONDENSED BRIEFING SCHEDULE

The Ex Parte Application of Plaintiff **Michael James Romero** for an **Order Shortening Time on Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Defendant’s Deposition; Request for Monetary Sanctions; and Request for Limited Leave After Discovery Cutoff; and for Condensed Briefing Schedule** (the “Application”) came on regularly for hearing on **November 13, 2025 at 9:00 a.m. in Dept. R12** of the above-entitled Court.

Plaintiff **Michael James Romero** appeared in support of the Application. Defendant **Megan Nicole Bentley** appeared through counsel, **Randal P. Hannah, Esq., Law Office of Randal P. Hannah**, or as otherwise reflected in the record.

The Court, having read and considered the Application, the **Declaration of Michael James Romero in Support of Ex Parte Application for Order Shortening Time**, the **Declaration of Michael James Romero Regarding Ex Parte Notice**, the underlying **Motion to Compel Defendant’s Deposition; Request for Monetary Sanctions; and Request for Limited Leave After Discovery Cutoff** (the “Motion”), any opposition and reply papers, and the arguments presented at the ex parte hearing, and good cause appearing, orders as follows:

### ORDER

1.  **Ex Parte Application Granted (Scheduling Relief Only).**  
    Plaintiff’s Ex Parte Application is **GRANTED** as set forth below. This Order provides **scheduling relief only**. The Court does **not** decide the merits of the Motion, rule on any request for sanctions, or grant or deny any discovery or evidentiary relief at this ex parte hearing.

2.  **Hearing on Underlying Motion Advanced.**  
    The hearing on Plaintiff’s **Motion to Compel Defendant’s Deposition; Request for Monetary Sanctions; and Request for Limited Leave After Discovery Cutoff**, previously set for **February 11, 2026 at 9:00 a.m. in Dept. R12**, is hereby **advanced** and **specially set** for hearing on:

    > **Date:** ____________________________  
    > **Time:** _________ a.m./p.m.  
    > **Dept.:** R12  
    > **Courthouse:** 8303 Haven Avenue, Rancho Cucamonga, California 91730

    The advanced hearing will be a regular noticed motion hearing on the Motion.

3.  **Service of Motion and This Order.**  
    Plaintiff shall **promptly serve** a copy of this Order, together with the Motion and all supporting papers (to the extent not previously served), on Defendant’s counsel of record, in a manner reasonably calculated to provide actual notice in advance of the advanced hearing date, and in accordance with the Code of Civil Procedure, the California Rules of Court, and any applicable local rules.

4.  **Opposition Deadline.**  
    Any **Opposition** to Plaintiff’s Motion shall be **filed and served** no later than **five (5) court days before** the advanced hearing date set by this Order, unless otherwise ordered by the Court.

5.  **Reply Deadline.**  
    Any **Reply** in support of Plaintiff’s Motion shall be **filed and served** no later than **two (2) court days before** the advanced hearing date, unless otherwise ordered by the Court.

6.  **Nature of Advanced Hearing.**  
    The advanced hearing on the Motion will proceed as a **regular noticed motion hearing**. The parties shall be prepared to address all issues raised by the Motion, including Plaintiff’s requests for relief under **Code of Civil Procedure sections 2025.450 and 2024.050** and any request for monetary sanctions, at that hearing. No such relief is granted or denied by this ex parte Order.

7.  **Further Orders.**  
    The Court may modify the foregoing deadlines or issue further orders regarding the scheduling or conduct of the hearing on the Motion on a showing of good cause or as justice may require.

IT IS SO ORDERED.
